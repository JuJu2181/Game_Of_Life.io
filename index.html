Conway's Game Of Life

  • Conway's Game Of Life is a Cellular Automation devised by British Mathematician John Horton Conway
  • Rules For Game:
    • Any live cell with less than two live neighbors will die due to underpopulation
    • Any live cell with more than three live neighbors will die due to overpopulation
    • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors will live to next generation
    • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors will become alive in next generation by reproduction

How to play ?

  • You can toggle the life of each cell by clicking on the cell. Green means alive
  • Before starting simulation atleast one cell should be alive. But if many cells are alive you will get better simulation. To start simulation press start simualtion
  • Once simulation starts, you can stop simulation process by clicking stop simulation
  • Reset will reset the game
  • You can also use generate randomly to generate random cells
